I'm a post-doctoral researcher/lecturer at University of Washington under Behcet Ackimese and Lillian Ratliff. My research is in control theory and optimization applied to complex cyber-physical systems involving competition. Through teaching linear algebra, optimization, control theory and estimation I also have a passion for math visualization and am working on a suite of web-based interactive visualizations for teaching linear algebra, optimization, control theory, and estimation.
